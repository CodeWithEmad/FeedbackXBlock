This XBlock allows you to collect student feedback on pieces of the course. This may be helpful either for course improvement, or to give students a chance to reflect on what they have done.

Please note that the text feedback needs to be analyzed as part of the edX research dumps. For now, we do not provide any kind of visual analytics associated with this block. We do display numbers of students giving specific feedback.

We do recommend matrix sampling. This can be done by using the randomized control trials framework.

We suggest asking specific questions which provide students clear guidance. For the Likert prompt, it is helpful to be very specific: Was this assignment easy to understand? Was the length appropriate? For the freeform response, you should provide students clear guidance on what kind of feedback you would like.

  • Example: Please provide us feedback on this section.
  • Example: Please rate your overall experience with this section.
